  • Systematic synthetic phonics (Phases 2‚Äì5)
  • Multisensory teaching methods to support all learners
  • Blending, segmenting, tricky words and fluency
  • Year 1 Phonics Screening Check preparation
  • Support for early readers, EAL learners and SEND

11+ Preparation

(Years 3 to 6)

We help children feel confident and capable in every aspect of 11+ English (grammar school and independent school routes).

  • Reading Comprehension: Strategies for retrieval, inference, vocabulary in context
  • Creative & Formal Writing: Structure, sentence variety, punctuation and vocabulary
  • SPaG: Grammar rules, sentence structures, punctuation, and spelling patterns
  • Verbal Reasoning: Vocabulary, logic, language patterns, and code-based tasks
  • Exam Strategy & Stamina: Time management, pacing and confidence building

Additional Subjects & Skills

  • Maths Support: Number, calculation, problem-solving, and 11+ Maths exam preparation
  • Non-Verbal Reasoning (NVR): Pattern recognition, logic, spatial awareness
  • Debate and Public Speaking: Building confident verbal communication and presentation skills
